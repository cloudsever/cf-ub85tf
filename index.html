What is Drake Bell's full name and background?

Drake Bell was born Jared Drake Bell on June 27, 1986, in Newport Beach, California. Drake's career began at the tender age of five when he appeared in commercials and small television roles. His big break came when he joined the cast of The Amanda Show in 1999. This sketch comedy series showcased his comedic talent and helped him gain recognition.

It featured the hilarious antics of two stepbrothers navigating their teenage years.
